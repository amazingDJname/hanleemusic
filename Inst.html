    portals, #1 - demo

    This installation uses a classic arcade game as a vehicle for soundscape generation.
    It is an attempt at removing players from their physically occupied space through gameplay,
    gameplay visuals, gameplay-resultant soundscapes, and the jarring disparity between all of those things.
